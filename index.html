How To Buy

Arm Yourself with a Wallet

Download a powerful wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet. Create your secure base, guard your recovery phrase like a weapon, and fortify with strong passwords. You’re setting up the foundation

Secure Your ETH Ammo

Buy Ethereum (ETH) for firepower — essential for taking down enemies and covering transaction costs. Grab it through your wallet or transfer from an exchange like Coinbase.

Enter the Battlefield

Launch Uniswap or another DEX, then link your wallet to infiltrate the trading zone. Confirm connections carefully. You’re ready to hunt down your token target; make sure your arsenal is primed, and your ETH’s in position to strike.

Strike and Conquer

In the DEX, load your ETH, then paste your target token’s contract address to lock in. Review the cost of attack (no gas fees), then click Swap. Confirm the mission in your wallet, await success, and claim victory as your token appears.
